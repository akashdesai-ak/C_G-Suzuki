Gixxer 250

Suzuki Gixxer 250 is a street bike available in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 2 colours. The Suzuki Gixxer 250 is powered by 249cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 26.13 bhp and a torque of 22.2 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Suzuki Gixxer 250 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Gixxer 250 bike weighs 156 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 12 liters.
